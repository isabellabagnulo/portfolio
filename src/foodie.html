Foodie is an innovative app to finally eliminate food waste. It allows you to always have memory of food in the pantry and of expiry dates, proposing ad hoc recipes based on your preferences/ dietary needs to finish up the perishing ingredients. It also allows you to add to your shopping list the used up ingredients.
